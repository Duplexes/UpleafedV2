What is Upleafed?

Upleafed is an organization that promotes mental, social, physical and spirtual wellbeing in workplaces. At first, the leaves of the wellness tree glow red. If a task is completed, the leaf turns green. Once the entire tree is green, the workplace will be rewarded. Completing the tasks requires collaboration. For more information, visit our website.
